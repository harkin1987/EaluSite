About éalú

Ealu is a classic side scrolling adventure created by you. The in-game level builder allows you to unleash your creative potential and share it with the world.

If creation isn't your thing you can jump in to online mode and experience other creations through 100 lives mode or the level browser

- Level Builder
- 60+ level components and game objects (With more on the way)
- Level browser
- 100 Lives Online mode
- Custom Physics for a true retro feel
